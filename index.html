Lyricle

Lyricle is a lyric guessing game where you try to guess the song from as little lyrics as possible. You'll start off with the first line of the song and if you guess the song incorrectly, it will give you another line. Six wrong guesses and you are out. Good luck :)

Instructions

  1. Choose a playlist
  2. Click "Play"
  3. Guess the song!
